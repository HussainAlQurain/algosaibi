A Shining Page in the History of the Kingdom

Our History

From pearling and farming in Huraimila to establishing one of the Kingdom's most respected business groups in the Eastern Province.

Early 1900s — Humble Beginnings

The Journey of Hamad bin Ahmad Algosaibi

Born in Huraymila, Sheikh Hamad began his journey with limited resources but strong determination. He worked in pearl diving and agriculture, building the foundation of his work ethic and his deep connection to the people of the Eastern Province.

His vision extended far beyond trade — he sought to build institutions that would serve the Kingdom and its people for generations to come, becoming a cornerstone of the Eastern Province's commercial and industrial development.

Milestones

The Beginning

Early 1900s

From Huraimila to Jubail

Originally born in Huraimila in Najd, Sheikh Hamad Algosaibi built his early livelihood through pearling and farming before expanding into trade.

1940

Establishment of the First Business

In 1940, Sheikh Hamad established Hamad Algosaibi Co. in the Eastern Province, setting the foundation for a lasting business legacy.

1940s

Support for Aramco Payroll

He became known for securing the silver coins used to pay Saudi employees who preferred local currency over U.S. dollars — a mark of deep community trust.

1940s–1950s

Eastern Province Chamber of Commerce

He helped establish and support the Eastern Province Chamber of Commerce during the region's early economic expansion.

1950s

Dedicated Aramco Warehouse

Sheikh Hamad strengthened ties with Aramco, established a warehouse dedicated to its operations, and became an early supplier of oil pipes and equipment.

Mid-1950s

Pepsi Cola & Industrial Expansion

Sheikh Hamad crowned his achievements by opening the first Pepsi Cola bottling factory in the Eastern Province — later expanding with his sons into Ahmad Hamad Algosaibi & Bros.

1960s onward

Six Decades of Diversification

The group expanded across manufacturing, energy, hospitality, media, and travel — building the diversified portfolio of 20+ companies across 6 sectors that exists today.

A legacy Saudi business group with diversified operations spanning manufacturing, energy, hospitality, transport and consumer business.
