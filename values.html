Our Values

The six values presented by Ahmad Hamad Algosaibi & Bros. Co. as the foundation for individual and collective action.

Our Values

The Group's Published Values

On its public profile, Ahmad Hamad Algosaibi & Bros. Co. identifies six values that guide how the business operates and how people work together.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in everything we do.

Safety

We strive to maintain the highest levels of safety, security, health, and environmental standards.

Fairness & Integrity

We strive for fairness and adhere to the highest ethical standards.

Trust

We place authority where the responsibility lies.

Accountability

We are accountable for all our actions.

Teamwork

We support each other and work together to achieve our business objectives successfully.

Values Statement

What These Values Emphasize

High Standards

The published statement places excellence alongside safety, security, health, and environmental standards.

Responsible Conduct

Fairness, integrity, trust, and accountability define how responsibility and authority are described.

Collective Performance

The statement also connects teamwork with mutual support and the successful achievement of business objectives.
