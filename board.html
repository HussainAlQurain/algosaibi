Principles of Governance

The board is guided by a deep sense of responsibility to every stakeholder — from employees and clients to the broader community of the Kingdom.

Open and honest communication with all stakeholders, built on consistent reporting and clear accountability at every level of the organization.

■

Integrity

Decisions grounded in ethical principles — upholding the values instilled by our founder and carried forward by every generation of leadership.

■
