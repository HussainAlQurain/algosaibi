Who We Are

Building Saudi Business Legacy

Commercially, the company became known for establishing one of the first warehouses dedicated to Saudi Aramco and supplying early locally purchased orders for steel pipes, tugboats, and tires.

Industrially, the group pioneered key ventures including the first Pepsi bottling factory in the Eastern Province, alongside foundational partnerships that supported the Kingdom's development.
