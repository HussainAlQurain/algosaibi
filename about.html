About Us

A Saudi Family Business Founded in 1940

Ahmad Hamad Algosaibi & Bros. Co. was established in 1940 in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia.

In its early commercial history, the Company is known for establishing the first warehouse dedicated solely to Saudi Aramco and for supplying Saudi Aramco's first locally purchased order for steel pipes, tugboats, and tires.

The group's historical profile also records its role in early industrial development in the Eastern Province, including the first electric power station and the Saudi cement plant.

Today, the portfolio presented on this website spans six sectors and 19 companies across hospitality & recreation, food & catering, manufacturing & industrial, energy & power, transport & logistics, and media & travel.

The Company's public profile also emphasizes social and environmental responsibility alongside support for community initiatives.
