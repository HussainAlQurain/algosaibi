Our History

Origins

The Story of Sheikh Hamad bin Ahmad Algosaibi

The history of Ahmad Hamad Algosaibi & Bros. Co. begins with Sheikh Hamad bin Ahmad Algosaibi, whose work and commercial experience laid the foundation for the family business established in the Eastern Province.

From those beginnings, the Company developed into a long-standing Saudi business presence linked to the commercial and industrial growth of the Eastern Province — from early warehousing and supply roles to operating companies and investments across the Kingdom today.

Heritage

Built Across Generations

Three threads run through the Group’s story: commercial foundations in the Eastern Province, early industrial participation, and the diversified portfolio that represents the business today.

01

Commercial Foundations

The Company is known for establishing the first warehouse dedicated solely to Saudi Aramco and for supplying Aramco’s first locally purchased order for steel pipes, tugboats, and tires.

02

Industrial Participation

The Group’s historical profile includes early industrial development in the Eastern Province, including participation in the first electric power station and the Saudi cement plant.

03

The Portfolio Today

Today the website portfolio includes 19 companies across hospitality, food, manufacturing, energy, transport, media, and travel.

Establishment

Eastern Province Foundations

The business was established in 1940 in the Eastern Province. In its early commercial history, the Company is known for establishing the first warehouse dedicated solely to Saudi Aramco and for supplying Saudi Aramco’s first locally purchased order for steel pipes, tugboats, and tires.
