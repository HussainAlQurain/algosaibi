Our History

From its establishment in the Eastern Province in 1940 to a present-day portfolio of companies and investments across six sectors.

Origins

The Story of Sheikh Hamad bin Ahmad Algosaibi

The history of Ahmad Hamad Algosaibi & Bros. Co. begins with Sheikh Hamad bin Ahmad Algosaibi, whose work and commercial experience laid the foundation for the family business established in the Eastern Province.

From those beginnings, the Company developed into a long-standing Saudi business presence linked to the commercial and industrial growth of the Eastern Province.

Milestones

Key Milestones

1940

Establishment in the Eastern Province

The business was established in 1940 in the Eastern Province. In its early commercial history, the Company is known for establishing the first warehouse dedicated solely to Saudi Aramco and for supplying Saudi Aramco's first locally purchased order for steel pipes, tugboats, and tires.

Early Industrial Development

The group's historical profile also records its role in early industrial development in the Eastern Province, including the first electric power station and the Saudi cement plant.

Growth Through Companies and Investments

Over the years, the business expanded through operating companies, partnerships, and investments in multiple sectors of the Saudi economy.
