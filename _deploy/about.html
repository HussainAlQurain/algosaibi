About Us

A Legacy of Commercial & Industrial Firsts

Commercially, the Company is known to have established the first warehouse dedicated solely to Saudi Aramco — supplying Aramco's first locally purchased order for steel pipes, tugboats, and tires.

Industrially, the Company pioneered a soft-drinks bottling factory in the mid-1950s and co-founded the first electric power station and Saudi cement plant in the Eastern Province of the Kingdom.

Over the decades, the enterprise has grown into successful businesses spanning manufacturing, trading, shipping, real estate, agriculture, travel services, media, and hospitality — with branches throughout the Kingdom.

Social and environmental responsibilities have remained central to the Company's strategy — reflected in its support for community welfare societies and charitable contributions in Saudi Arabia and abroad.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To create lasting economic value through diversified, ethically-led business — honoring the pioneering legacy of our founders, contributing to the Kingdom's growth, and creating opportunity for our people and communities across Saudi Arabia.

Vision

To be the most trusted and respected Saudi family business group — one that honors its roots, empowers its people, upholds the highest standards of integrity, and builds enduring value across every industry we serve.
